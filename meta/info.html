Pro Micro Template

This project template is the basis of an expansion board for the Pro Micro form factor. It includes a PCB edge matching the outline of the Pro Micro PCB with connectors placed correctly. It also includes USB-C and optional Stemma QT/Qwiic.

Note: due to the fact that the official 3D library does not include "long" or "stacking" female headers, the default Connector_PinSocket_2.54mm models were used.
